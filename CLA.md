## How to sign

- **Individuals:** complete the **Individual CLA** below.
- **Contributing on behalf of an employer/company:** have an authorized
  signatory complete the **Corporate CLA** below.

Signature is collected automatically when you open your first pull request
(see [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md)). By submitting a contribution and
indicating your agreement (e.g., via the CLA assistant check or a signed-off
commit referencing this document), you accept the terms that apply to you.
